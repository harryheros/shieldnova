! ============================================================================
! ShieldNova - Optional / Aggressive
! Stricter privacy rules that MAY affect some app functionality.
!
! WARNING: These rules block domains that serve both tracking AND
! legitimate features. Only use if you understand the trade-offs.
! ============================================================================

! --- Social Platform Tracking (may break embedded content) ---
||graph.facebook.com^                     ! Facebook Graph API - breaks FB Login & sharing
||connect.facebook.net^                   ! Facebook SDK - breaks FB Login buttons on websites
||platform.twitter.com^                   ! Twitter/X embeds - breaks embedded tweets
||platform.linkedin.com^                  ! LinkedIn embeds - breaks embedded posts

! --- Deep Linking (may break "open in app" links) ---
||branch.io^                              ! Branch - breaks deep links & share URLs
||app.link^                               ! Branch - breaks app-to-app links

! --- Customer-Facing Services (may break support & notifications) ---
||hubspot.com^                            ! HubSpot - breaks sites hosted on HubSpot
||intercom.io^                            ! Intercom - breaks in-app customer support chat
||braze.com^                              ! Braze - breaks push notifications in some apps
||customer.io^                            ! Customer.io - breaks email unsubscribe links

! --- Error Monitoring (may cause app instability) ---
||sentry.io^                              ! Sentry - some apps crash if reporting fails
||bugsnag.com^                            ! Bugsnag - some apps check-in on startup
||newrelic.com^                           ! New Relic - may affect app performance monitoring
||nr-data.net^                            ! New Relic - telemetry collection

! --- Bot Detection (WILL break login on some sites) ---
||arkoselabs.com^                         ! Arkose Labs - blocks CAPTCHA on EA, Microsoft, etc.
||perimeterx.net^                         ! PerimeterX - blocks access to many e-commerce sites

! --- China Push Services (WILL cause missed notifications) ---
||getui.com^                              ! Getui - push notifications for many China apps
||igexin.com^                             ! Igexin (Getui) - push SDK
||jpush.cn^                               ! JPush (Aurora) - push notifications
